A peer-to-peer chat app. No accounts, no servers, just you and your Bros.

## How to Use

**Host:**
1. Open HelloBros
2. Enter your name
3. Click Host
4. Share the code shown at the top with your friend

**Join:**
1. Open HelloBros
2. Enter your name
3. Enter the code your friend sent
4. Click Join

## Requirements
- Nothing. All Packages are bundled inside.
